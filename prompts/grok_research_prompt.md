# Fundbot External Research Prompt

Use this ONLY when fundbot says external qualitative context is missing.
Do not ask for price-performance conclusions; fundbot will handle quantitative ranking.

Prompt:

"Analyze the current Turkish investment fund / TEFAS context for the following theme or fund codes: [CODES/THEME]. Separate verified facts, market narratives, social sentiment, hype/rumors, and unknowns. Focus on fund-specific structural issues, management changes, liquidity/availability problems, regulatory constraints, and macro regime context. Do not give a buy/sell recommendation. Cite sources or explicitly say if you cannot verify a claim."

When you bring the answer back into fundbot, it is treated as tertiary context only.
